About

Hi! I'm Mekal Covic, also known online as Valoeghese. I'm a kiwi who loves music, anime, programming, fantasy novels, and, in general, learning and being able to create stuff for others to enjoy.
 I am mostly known online for my work in the field of Minecraft Modding. Some projects I have worked on in this field include Cosmetica, my realistic terrain generator "Naturverbunden: Terrain", and the Cursed Legacy Project, a toolchain which enables modding Minecraft Beta 1.7.3 with Fabric Modloader. However, these are only but a small part of what I do.
 I also compose music. A selection of my music, primarily compositions for projects I have worked on, have been uploaded to my youtube channel.
