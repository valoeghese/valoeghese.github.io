About

Hi! I'm Mekal Covic, also known online as Valoeghese. I'm a kiwi who loves music, anime, and programming, and, in general, learning and being able to create stuff for others to enjoy.
 I am mostly known for my work in the field of Minecraft Modding. Some projects I have worked on in this field include my realistic terrain generator "Naturverbunden Terrain", and the Cursed Legacy Project, a toolchain which enables modding Minecraft Beta 1.7.3 with Fabric Modloader. However, these are only but a small part of what I do.
 I also compose music. A selection of my music, especially those compositions that relate to other projects I have worked on, have been uploaded to my youtube channel.
